Overview

Purpose

As the owner of the rafting business, the purpose of the website is to establish an online presence to attract new customers, provide information about our rafting trips, and facilitate booking.

Audience

Our target audience includes adventure enthusiasts, families, and groups looking for exciting outdoor experiences. Three scenario questions:

  • Q: Are you interested in thrilling outdoor activities?
  • A: Yes, our website provides information about exciting rafting trips.
  • Q: Are you looking for family-friendly adventures?
  • A: Absolutely, our site offers fun and safe experiences for families.
  • Q: Do you want to book a group adventure?
  • A: Yes, our website facilitates group bookings for unforgettable experiences.
